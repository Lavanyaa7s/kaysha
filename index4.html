Our Story

CRAFTING MEMORABLE FOOD EXPERIENCES AT KAYSHA

We take pride in delivering an exceptional dining experience through the highest standards of food quality and South Indian heritage.

Our cooks prepare every briyani with precision, maintaining a harmonious balance of Tanjore spice, fragrance, and generous portions. As a proudly women-owned and family-run kitchen, we follow uncompromising hygiene and freshness protocols—ensuring our kitchen operates with excellence at every single step.

Our Vision

To create an exceptional dining experience that combines exquisite flavours, warm hospitality, and a welcoming ambience right in historic Banda Hilir. We aim to be a place where memorable moments are shared over outstanding banana leaf meals.
